About Us

Girls can Malawi is an initiative that aims to empower girls through access to quality education. We believe that through education, girls can contribute significantly to the nation's development, girls can end the cycle of poverty, girls can have better health outcomes and gender equality.

For us, investing in girl's education is not just a matter of social justice, it is a strategic investment in future of Malawi.

"WHY GIRLS: Girls have historically experienced prejudice and had fewer chances in a variety of fields, including healthcare, work, and education."

One of the most vulnerable groups in society is girls. Making girls a priority is a calculated attempt to address and correct these differences, advancing gender parity and empowering girls, contesting preconceptions and traditional gender roles.
