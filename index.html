About Us

Who We Are

Girls can Malawi is an initiative that aims to empower girls through access to quality education. we believe that through education, girls can contribute significantly to the nation’s development, girls can end the cycle of poverty, girls can have better health outcomes and gender equality. For us, investing in girl’s education is not just a matter of social justice, it is a strategic investment in future of Malawi.

Why Girls? +

We believe that through education, girls can contribute significantly to the nation’s development, end the cycle of poverty, have better health outcomes, and achieve gender equality. Investing in girls' education is not just a matter of social justice; it is a strategic investment in the future of Malawi.
