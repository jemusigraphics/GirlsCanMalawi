Volunteer with Us

Be a Part of the Change. Empower Girls in Malawi.

At Girls Can Malawi, we believe that empowering girls through education is essential for a brighter and more equitable future. Our mission is to provide girls with the tools, knowledge, and support they need to overcome social, cultural, and economic barriers. As a volunteer, you can play a vital role in helping us make a lasting impact.

Why Volunteer with Girls Can Malawi?

  • Promote Gender Equality: Help us in our journey to close the gender gap and create equal opportunities for girls.
  • Support Education Initiatives: Contribute to sending girls back to school and providing necessary school supplies.
  • Encourage Menstrual Hygiene: Participate in our programs to educate girls on menstrual health, distribute reusable sanitary products, and combat stigma.
  • Mentor Young Girls: Become a role model by guiding girls in areas like education, health, leadership, and entrepreneurship.

Volunteer Roles

Education Support

Assist in our initiatives to encourage girls to return to school by helping distribute school essentials like uniforms, notebooks, and pens.

Menstrual Hygiene Advocacy

Work with us in raising awareness on menstrual health, teaching girls to make reusable pads, and supporting hygiene education.

Mentorship Programs

Provide mentorship to young girls, guiding them in their educational and career paths, and helping them build confidence and leadership skills.

Community Outreach

Engage with local communities to foster awareness and support for girls' education and empowerment, helping to shift cultural perceptions.

How to Get Involved

If you are passionate about making a difference and want to support girls in achieving their dreams, join us! Volunteers are welcome from all backgrounds. Together, we can empower the next generation of Malawian girls.
